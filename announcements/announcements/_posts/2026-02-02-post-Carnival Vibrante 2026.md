<h2>You’re warmly invited!</h2>
<p>
<br>Our school’s Public Funfair, Carnival Vibrante,&nbsp; is just around the
corner, and we would love to welcome the general public, alumni, friends,
and supporters to join the fun!</p>
<p>
<br>Expect exciting games, a wide variety of delicious food, beautiful jewellery
and handicrafts, and fun services like henna, hair tying, and more.</p>
<p>
<br>Tickets will be available on the day.&nbsp;For those who wish to purchase
Funfair coupons in advance, they are available at our Accounts Office from
Monday to Friday, 9.00am to 5.00pm.
<br>
<br>Come along, bring your loved ones, and enjoy a joyful day of community,
colour, and connection.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
