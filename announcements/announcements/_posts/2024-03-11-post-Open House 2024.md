<h4>Do join us at our Open House on <strong>Saturday, 6 April</strong>, from 8am to 2pm!</h4>
<p>We invite you to explore our beautiful campus. Discover our innovative
and engaging academic programmes through the Curriculum Showcase. Find
out more about the diverse range of CCAs we offer by attending the concerts
and visiting all the CCAs at the CCA Showcase. Attend the various school
talks and live Q and A sessions where you will get to meet the Principal
and Vice Principals. Find out more about student admission and talent development.
Sign up for an exciting school tour to explore our campus. Look out for
the surprise performances by our students and participate in quizzes to
win some prizes. You will have opportunities to interact with the students
to understand what it means to be a Nanyang Girl. So visit us to learn
about our holistic programmes which we offer to nurture our students into
women of character, empowered learners, and agents of change.</p>
<p>We look forward to seeing you at our Open House on Saturday, 6 April!</p>
<p>Please take public transport. There will be no parking provided.</p>
<p><strong>To find out more, please visit our&nbsp;<a href="https://openhousenygh.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">OPEN HOUSE WEBSITE</a>.</strong>
</p>
