Special Assistance Plan (SAP) school that provides an all-round holistic
education to the students.</p>

education and a global perspective.</p>
<p>Nanyang Girls’ High School invites applications from qualified, highly
motivated individuals, to be part of the dynamic, committed and innovative
workforce.</p>

<h5 id="r-ict">ICT MANAGER</h5>
<p>Key Responsibilities:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>Manage the school’s ICT infrastructure, including networks, servers, Wi-Fi systems, and endpoint devices;</li>
<li>Oversee asset management, procurement processes (e.g., GeBIZ), and vendor coordination;</li>
<li>Ensure cybersecurity risk management and compliance with MOE or international data protection standards;</li>
<li>Lead and coordinate school-wide ICT projects to support digital transformation initiatives;</li>
<li>Manage and maintain learning management systems (e.g., Google Workspace for Education) and ensure their effective integration into teaching and learning;</li>
<li>Provide technical support and training to teachers and staff on ICT tools and educational technology practices;</li>
<li>Track and report ICT funding utilisation and data analytics to support planning and decision-making;</li>
<li>Liaise with MOE HQ ICT Divisions, NCS, and relevant vendors to ensure smooth ICT operations;</li>
<li>Develop and implement ICT strategies aligned with the school’s overall goals and direction;</li>
<li>Support digital learning initiatives, promoting the adoption of innovative EdTech tools; and</li>
<li>Any other adhoc duties.</li>
</ul>
<p>Requirements:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>Diploma or Degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or a related discipline;</li>
<li>Professional certifications in network administration or cybersecurity (e.g., CompTIA, Cisco, or equivalent) preferred;</li>
<li>Minimum 3 years of relevant experience in ICT systems administration, infrastructure, or educational technology, preferably within an educational institution;</li>
<li>Analytical, organised, and collaborative, with strong problem-solving and communication skills; and</li>
<li>Passionate about leveraging technology to enhance teaching and learning. Background in education technology or digital learning environments.</li>
</ul>
